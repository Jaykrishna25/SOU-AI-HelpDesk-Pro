# User Manual (Student & Faculty)

## Signing In
1. Open the platform and click **Launch**.
2. Enter your **Login ID** (enrollment/staff number) and **Birthdate**, or click a demo role button.
3. Click **Sign In** — you land on your role dashboard.

## Student Portal
- **Dashboard** — CGPA, attendance %, pending fees, current semester at a glance.
- **Timetable** — daily/weekly lectures with subject, faculty, time, classroom, building.
- **Attendance** — subject-wise bars and overall %.
- **Fees** — total / paid / pending; download receipts.
- **Results** — internal & external marks, SGPA/CGPA.
- **Exams** — datesheet, venue, seat number.
- **AI Assistant** — click the floating bot (bottom-right). Ask about exams, fees, attendance.
  If the AI is confident it answers instantly; otherwise it raises a ticket and you'll be notified.

## Faculty Portal
- **Attendance** — mark/update per lecture, generate reports.
- **Content** — upload notes, PPTs, assignments, videos.
- **Exam Duties** — view invigilation schedule.
- **Salary** — status, history, receipts.
- **Tickets** — resolve or escalate assigned tickets.

## Tracking a Ticket
Open **AI Assistant / Tickets** to see status (Open → Assigned → Resolved) and every notification.
